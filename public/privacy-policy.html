Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how All Time Market ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Location Information: We may collect your precise or approximate location to provide accurate delivery services.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including crash reports and diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including processing and delivering your orders.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service or your order status.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To monitor the usage of our Service and improve user experience.

3. Data Security and Deletion

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All data is encrypted in transit.

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. You can do this by contacting our support team or using the account deletion feature within the App.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@alltimemarket.com.
